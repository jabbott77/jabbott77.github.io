Target Audience

Description

The target audience would be those who would like to know what is going to happen weather-wise in their area. Understanding what the weather will be like in their area will help people plan out their day and week. Any people currently living in these areas or planning to visit would beneift greatly in visiting this site.

Personas

1. A 42 year old mother has been working on her garden for years in preparation for the local fair to show off her beautiful veggies. Certain weeks she needs to know if there will be enough rain to water her crops and if there'll be enough sun to help grow. She would benefit by visiting this site to plan for crops accordingly.

2. A 34 year old Pee Wee football coach is trying to determine what the weather will be like for this upcoming week. Based on the weather, the team can understand what days they can practice and if their scheduled game is on the best day play football. This coach and many other teams would be able to decide which days are the most safe and fun to play one of the best sports created.

Scenarios

  • I need to know what the weather will be like so I can decide if want to go for a run later today.
  • I want to go fishing with my children this weekend, but I don't want to go if it'll rain all day.
  • My friend wants to go sledding on the holiday and we need to know if how much snow will fall and if it'll be enough to sled on.
  • She is a bit scared because there are big storm clouds gathering. Are we safe? Should we go somewhere?
  • I don't know if I should water my plants tomorrow. I heard it was going to rain, but I'm not sure.
