Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide valuable information regarding to weather in the certain areas. News about weather conditions are updated regularly along with helpful articles about the history of different places and its weather. The public needs to be informed about the changing weather conditions of their residence and there is no better resource to use than Weather the Weather!
