Celestial Comfort

Full-time Missionaries/Guest Rooms

We appreciate the hard work of all full-time missionaries and temple patrons. That is why we have provided high quality rooms and suites for your stay to thank and comfort you for your hard work in the Lord's House. Full-time Missionaries will have discounted prices for each type of room, just be sure to talk with a representative on our contact page to discuss the different discounts for you to use.

Single/Double/Triple/Quad

These rooms provide comfort at a cheap price. You can fit up to 5-6 people in a quad with a pull out sofa. This picture shows a double, but we have plenty of each room, just be sure to call ahead to reserve a room. Rates: Single Room - $80 per night + $25 for each bed added depending on how many guests there are.

Queen

This room is even deserving for a queen. A perfect room to prepare you for your worship in the temple. This room can fit up to 3-4 people with a pull out sofa. This room also has a nice view of the city in certain locations, so be sure to call ahead to reserve a room. Rates: Queen Room - $150 per night

King

A true room for a king. This room boasts a luxurious bed and exquisite bath and shower. This room can fit up to 3-4 people with a pull out sofa. This room has a beautiful view of the temple in all locations, so be sure to call ahead to reserve a room. Rates: King Room - $200 per night

The Celestial Suite

The best rooms that we have to offer. The Celestial Suite holds multiple bedrooms which can hold about 5-6 people(great for families). This picture shows one of the bedrooms and all the bedrooms are connected to a kitchen leading a living room. There are three majestic bathrooms and the suite has an amazing view of both the city and the temple. This is simply the best room that you will ever stay in, so be sure to call ahead to reserve a room. Rates: The Celestial Suite - $350 per night
