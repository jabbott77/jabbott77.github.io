Weddings

Accomodations

We want to make sure that your wedding is special and convenient. We have three different plans to choose from so you can have a wide variety of options to choose from. Once you choose a plan, please refer to the contact page and call or email or headquarters requesting a reservation for one of the halls.

  • Gold Package: Reserve our largest reception hall for one night. Professional wedding band provided. Personal butlers attending to clean and serve food. Personal hotel limo and driver for entire day. Choice of one other form of entertainment from local area to perfom(discuss with assistant for details). Lights, photographer, and magician provided. Refer to Food and Setup sections on this page regarding those specific options. Total Cost: $6,000


  • Silver Package: Reserve our medium reception hall for one night. Semi-professional wedding band provided. Personal hotel shuttle and driver for entire day. Lights, photographer, and monkey provided. Refer to Food and Setup sections on this page regarding those specific options. Total Cost: $4,000


  • Bronze Package: Reserve our small reception hall for one night. Mediocre wedding band provided. Personal hotel taxi and driver for entire day. Lights, photographer, and clown provided. Refer to Food and Setup sections on this page regarding those specific options. Total Cost: $2,000

    • Food

    During your wedding, we would like to prepare for you the most exquiste food that you will ever taste. You can choose from three food themes to serve at your wedding reception. Once you choose a plan, please refer to the contact page and call or email or headquarters requesting a reservation for one of the food plans. Please contact a representative on how many guests will be in attendance.

  • Italian Pleasure: This meal will provide lasaguna, linguini, spaghetti, breadsticks, Caesar salads, and Gelato. Meal includes personal pan pizzas for each guest as well. Total Cost: $1,000


  • Western Wonder: This meal will provide steaks, BBQ ribs, angus burgers, grilled chicken, cornbread, Caesar salads, and Ice Cream Meal includes Chicken Nuggets for each guest as well. Total Cost: $1,000


  • Thai Delight: This meal will provide Pad Thai, sliced/minced spicy beef and pork, sticky and normal rice, Shrimp curry, fried pork with basil, noodles, Thai salads, and Mango Sticky Rice. Meal includes Thai-native fruits for each guest as well. Total Cost: $1,000

    Setup

    For your wedding day, we are happy to set you up with all these previous accomodations. With every wedding, a couple typically has a theme to choose from. We have many options of different themes to choose from. If you wish to look over these themes, please call our headquarters and a representative will be happy to go over the options with you. If you wish to go forward with your own personal theme, then please call our headquarters and a wedding designer will be happy to go over the details of how to make your idea a reality for your special day. Extra fees may be included depending on options chosen or personal theme for reception.
